Road From a Production Engineer to Full Stack Developer Student

This is how my career changed to front-end development at 31 and after a 7 years of work experience, starting with no relevant background experience or degree. I used self-directed study , I applied and was accepted into a scholarship at Gomycode.

“ I’m sitting in a coffee shop in the heart of Tunis, having a cup of coffee, tapping away at my laptop while it's raining outside. In a few moments I will walk into my first day as a Full Stack developer Student. Just one Week ago I was a Production Engineer, at a multinational company , who knows nothing about programming, and now I’m here. How did this happened?”

I wrote those excited words a few weeks ago as I was getting ready to start my first day on my new Challenge.
